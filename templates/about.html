About Us

Shelter wants to be your one stop place to get the latest updates on outdoor gear and gadgets. We give you the chance to share your latest gear findings with the community and explore the hottest trends in outdoor equipment.

Who are we?

We are 4 close friends that share the desire to spend as much time outside as possible. Our interests range from bird watching, wave and kite surfing, fishing, hiking, running, traveling, camping and climbing to cooking, making smores over a campfire, studying sunsets and much much more. With the love for the outdoors and an active lifestyle we want to connect to like-minded awesome people and build a caring, sustainable and inspiring community

What do we want to accomplish?

We love the outdoors and enjoying life under the blue sky. Based on this love we are always interessted in the newest innovation, the hottest trends or the most enduring and useful gear. Sitting at the campfire and talking to friends, family and other campsite visitors always reveals new ideas and viewpoints which we try to capture. This campfire chat about great gear or gadgets is what we try to bring to the web. We love the energy of the outdoor community and want to create a space where anyone can find inspiration or real-life tips on gear experiences. Therefore we all can benefit and spend our hard earned money for things that are time-tested and reliable.
